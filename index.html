Blackjack

Master the art of 21. Challenge the dealer in this classic casino game where strategy meets luck.

The Basics

Beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over. Everyone starts with two cards, but only one of the dealer's cards is visible.

Card Values

  • Face cards (J, Q, K) = 10
  • Aces = 1 or 11
  • Number cards = Face value

Your Moves

  • "Hit" to take another card
  • "Stand" to keep your current hand
  • Going over 21 means you bust

Dealer Rules

  • Must hit on 16 or below
  • Must stand on 17 or above
  • Reveals hidden card after you stand
The House Always Wins
